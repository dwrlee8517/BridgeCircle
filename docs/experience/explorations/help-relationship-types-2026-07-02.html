Exploration · ADR 0010 — horizontal help

Five ways two members meet, and how each one talks

Mentorship stops being an identity and becomes a commitment tier. Roles attach to a single exchange, never to a person. This exploration mocks each relationship type from both sides — because every gate, decline, and thread has to lower the barrier for the asker and protect the helper.

status exploration — not shipped · grounds ADR 0010 (proposed) + ADR 0003 gating split · voice voice-guidelines.md v1 · tokens Civic Editorial · 2026-07-02
The map

Two lanes, two gates

Everything peer-to-peer runs through one of two gates, and the gates are deliberately different. Asks are one-sided — a helper opts in once, and any member can approach them through the buffer. Friendship is two-sided — both people accept before a free channel opens. ADR 0010 keeps this split; collapsing it was explicitly rejected.

The help lane — one ask object, 4 directions

Scoped exchanges. The buffer does the social work.
gate: helper opt-in · one-sided
junior → seniorThe classic. Maren ('22) asks Jane ('08) a quick question.
senior → juniorThe reverse. David ('98) asks Priya ('21) about agent tooling. Topic relevance outranks class year. new in 0010
peer ↔ peerLateral. Two '22s compare notes — nobody is the expert. new in 0010
helper → askerSupply side. Grace sees an open ask and offers to help. new in 0010
Every direction is the same ask object with a commitment tier: quick (a few exchanges) or ongoing (paced, capacity-capped). Threads are scoped to the ask.

The friendship lane — one channel, no roles

Open-ended relationship. No buffer scaffolding needed once inside.
gate: mutual accept · two-sided
member ⇄ memberEither person sends the request; DM opens only after both say yes.
No commitment tiers, no expiry, no coach, no decline reasons. The two-sided gate is the protection — everything after it is just a conversation. An unanswered request fades quietly; the sender is never shown a rejection.

Type 5 · offer to help (helper-initiated)

Grace sees an open ask and raises her hand

The supply side of the same object — new in 0010. Standing asks already exist (count-only, 14-day TTL); this surfaces them to well-matched helpers on /help and lets the helper start. The buffer flips with the direction: now it's the asker who can pass quietly.

Type 6 · friendship → DM (the other lane)

Maren and Iris become friends — and the scaffolding disappears

After comparing notes, Maren and Iris want to just… talk. That's the friendship lane: a two-sided gate (mutual accept), then a plain, open-ended DM. No tiers, no expiry, no coach, no decline reasons — the mutual gate already did the protecting.

What marks this type
Mutual accept before any channel opens · unanswered requests fade quietly, no rejection surfaced · thread has no ask header, no lifecycle chrome · this is the one relationship that persists as a relationship.
Iris's side — the two-sided gate
